Pause & Wait

Stop. Do not text, phone, talk or write. This can be an opportunity. Become curious. Discover!


Observe & Watch

Observe the sensations. Where is the feeling in your body? What does it feel like? What colour is it? Is it changing? Watch it change.


Avoid

Avoid the story that we tell ourselves about ourselves, about the other person and about what happened.
